Emotional Intelligence

Mindfulness can be descibed as:

"The ability to monitor one’s own and other’s feelings and emotions, to discriminate among them and to use this information to guide one’s thinking and actions.” - Salovey and Mayer 1990

There are 3 Steps of Emotional Intelligence

  • Mindfullness
  • Self Knowledge and Mastery
  • Creating Useful Mental Habits

How is EQ Different to IQ

EQ measures ones social and emotional competencies, whereas IQ measures an individuals academic competency and reasoning ability.

Why is EQ Important?

A high emotional intelligence helps you build strong relationships, reduce team stress and improve job satisfaction.
